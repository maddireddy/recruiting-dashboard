## Presentation Delivery Notes

### Timing:
- Total Duration: 45-60 minutes
- Slides 1-10: 15 minutes (Introduction & Overview)
- Slides 11-19: 20 minutes (Feature Deep-Dive)
- Slides 20-25: 10 minutes (Implementation & ROI)
- Slide 26: 10 minutes (Live Demo)
- Slides 27-30: 5-10 minutes (Closing & Q&A)

### Presentation Tips:
1. **Start Strong:** Use storytelling - share a recruitment pain point they'll relate to
2. **Focus on Benefits:** Not features - show how it solves their problems
3. **Use Real Examples:** Customer stories, real metrics, actual screenshots
4. **Interactive Demo:** Let them drive - ask what they want to see
5. **Address Objections:** Proactively handle common concerns
6. **Strong Close:** Clear next steps with urgency

### Customization for Different Audiences:
- **Startups:** Emphasize speed to market, low cost, ease of use
- **Agencies:** Focus on bench sales, workflow automation, ROI
- **Enterprises:** Highlight security, compliance, scalability, integrations
- **Existing Companies:** Lead with migration success stories, gap analysis

### Follow-up Materials:
- Leave-behind: One-page summary + pricing sheet
- Email: Recording + proposal + ROI calculator
- Next step: Schedule follow-up call within 48 hours
